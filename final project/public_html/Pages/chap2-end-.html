Our story has reached its end.


"The text in this block will be in the Native Mi'kmaw language"
There will also be an audio reading of the text.

(Replay) Our story begins down by the river. It is a sunny day and I decided to go to the river to do some fishing.
I caught 5 trout. This was enough for my family to have a big feast. Down the river I saw a wounded Bear.
I saw that the bear was weak and hungry, so I decided to Give fish.
I gave the bear 2 fish, and he began to eat. After eating the fish, the bear began to walk away. Looking back the bear nodded its head at me, and I was happy. I went home and told my family The truth.
My family was proud that I helped the wounded bear, but they told me to be careful next time because it may be dangerous to feed wounded animals. I made a new friend that day with the bear and still had enough fish for my family to eat.

The Mi'kmaq Storybook.
